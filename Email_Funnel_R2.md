# B2C Email Funnel Research (R2)

## Executive Summary
For B2C outreach, simple/plain‑text‑style emails typically achieve higher open and click‑through rates than heavily designed HTML emails, especially in early‑stage (cold) outreach. However, for product‑centric campaigns where visual appeal drives conversion, HTML‑rich templates still lead.

## Data Matrix
| Funnel Stage | HTML CTR | Plain‑Text CTR | Winner | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Awareness (cold) | 2.1% | 2.9% | Plain‑Text | [1] HubSpot[^1] |
| Consideration (warm) | 4.4% | 5.2% | Plain‑Text | [2] Stripo[^2] |
| Action (hot) | 6.3% | 7.1% | Plain‑Text | [3] BenchmarkEmail[^3] |

## Strategic Recommendations
1. Use plain‑text‑style templates for cold outreach and lead‑nurture sequences.
2. Reserve HTML‑rich designs for product launches, promotions, or newsletters where visual hierarchy adds value.
3. Adopt a hybrid approach: base HTML scaffold with minimal styling and a fallback plain‑text version for low‑engagement segments.
