# 25 Conversations Sprint — Outreach to 50 ReviewTap Customers

**Objective**: Talk to 50 warm ReviewTap customers (know you, never spoke to Sam) → 25 conversations → warm leads for Vantage.

## Funnel

| Stage | Target |
|---|---|
| WhatsApp openers sent (Day 1) | 25 |
| Replies (~50%+) | 13 |
| Quality conversations (2+ exchanges, substantive) | 8–10 |
| Warm leads for Vantage | 5 |
| Vantage signups | 3 |

**"Conversation" definition**: 2+ exchanges where the lead gives a substantive answer to a follow-up question. Not one-word replies. Not "thanks." Screenshot evidence for each.

## Script Pattern

One pattern for all 25. Jacques referral is subtle, not featured.

1. **Opener**: Lead with a specific pain SA business owners face (admin overload, WhatsApp chaos). Ask one question. Soft reference to "we've spoken before through ReviewTap" — not "Jacques said."
2. **Nudge** (Day 3, non-responders only): Brief, friendly check-in. Option to opt out. One nudge max.
3. **Deep-dive** (engaged leads): Follow up on their pain point. Offer 1–2 specific insights. Invite to Thursday 7pm Vantage session.
4. **Handoff** (warm leads): Send lead magnet link (10 AI Prompts). Offer free first month of Vantage. Invite to Thursday session.

## Timeline

- **Day 1**: Send all 25 openers (not staggered)
- **Day 3**: Nudge non-responders
- **Day 5**: Close loop, report results to Jacques with screenshots and numbers

## Vantage Offer for Leads

- First month free (founding member pricing)
- Thursday 7pm live session invite
- Lead magnet link: the 10 AI Prompts page (to be deployed)
- No pushy selling — lead magnet + session invite is the handoff
