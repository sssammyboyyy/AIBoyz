# Conversations Sprint — Outreach to ReviewTap Customers

**Objective**: Talk to 50 warm ReviewTap customers (know you, never spoke to Sam) → conversations → warm leads for Vantage.

**Scope (per your request)**: Starting with 10 openers Day 1. If the pattern works, we scale to the remaining 40.

## Funnel

| Stage | Target |
|---|---|
| WhatsApp openers sent (Day 1) | 10 |
| Replies (~50%+) | 5 |
| Quality conversations (2+ exchanges, substantive) | 2–3 |
| Warm leads for Vantage | 2 |
| Vantage signups | 1–2 |

**"Conversation" definition**: 2+ exchanges where the lead gives a substantive answer to a follow-up question. Not one-word replies. Not "thanks." Screenshot evidence for each.

## Scripts

One pattern for all 10. Your referral is subtle — "we've spoken before through ReviewTap," not "Jacques said."

### 1. Opener (Day 1)

Sent via WhatsApp. Pain-first, one question, soft ReviewTap reference.

> Hi [Name], Sam here — we've chatted before through ReviewTap. Quick one: when it comes to running your business, what's the one thing that eats more of your time than it should?

No hard sell. No AI mention. Just a question that opens the door. If they reply substantively, follow up with the deep-dive. If not, nudge on Day 3.

### 2. Nudge (Day 3, non-responders only)

Brief. Single nudge. Give them an out.

> Hi [Name], just circling back in case this got buried. No stress if it's bad timing — I'll leave it with you. If you ever want to chat about saving time on the admin side, I'm here.

### 3. Deep-dive (for engaged leads)

Follow their reply. Offer one insight relevant to what they mentioned. Then invite.

> That makes sense — a lot of business owners I've spoken to say the same thing about [their pain point]. One thing that's been helping: using AI to handle the repetitive stuff so you can focus on the work that actually needs you.
>
> I'm running a free live session Thursday 7pm where I'll show exactly how. Want me to send you the link?

### 4. Handoff (for warm leads)

Send the lead magnet. Offer first month free. Thursday invite.

> Glad it's useful. Here's a link to 10 AI prompts built for SA business owners — copy-paste, no fluff: https://sssammyboyyy.github.io/AIBoyz/
>
> Also: first month of Vantage is free for founding members. Thursday 7pm session walks through the first 3 prompts live. Keen?
