Getting Started with AI

Mobile instructions — 4 steps to using AI prompts on your phone.

1

Download ChatGPT (free)

No paid plan needed. The free tier runs most AI prompts. Get the app or use the web version:

iPhone Android Web browser
2

Create an account

Sign up with Google or email. Takes 30 seconds. Free tier is all you need. You can also use Claude, Gemini, or Copilot — the prompts work with any AI tool.

3

Copy a prompt

Pick the prompt that matches your biggest headache right now. Copy it, paste into ChatGPT, then fill in the bits in [brackets] with your specific details.

4

Refine the output

ChatGPT gives you a first draft. Tell it what to change: "Shorten this," "Make it more formal," "Write it in plain English." The more specific you are, the better it gets.
